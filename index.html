About This App

Take control of your finances with powerful budgeting tools, transaction tracking, and insightful analytics designed specifically for students.

Our app helps students:

  • Track daily spending and income easily.
  • Set monthly budget limits and monitor overspending.
  • Visualize spending trends over the last 7 days.
  • Analyze which categories consume the most funds.
  • Search and filter transactions quickly using regex or keywords.
  • Export or import data for backup or analysis.
  • Use smart alerts for duplicates, invalid inputs, or budget overruns.

Empowering students to make smarter financial decisions, understand their habits, and save more effectively.
